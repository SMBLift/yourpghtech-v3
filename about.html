About Your Pgh Tech

Who we are

Your Pgh Tech LLC has been providing custom IT solutions to local small businesses for over 15 years. We are happy to partner with both established companies or those that are just starting. We strive to build long-lasting relationships with all clients and really pride ourselves on being responsive, reliable and trustworthy. We are dedicated to working together to find the ideal IT solution for your constantly changing business needs.

The founder is a Baldwin High School graduate. He has a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems and Master of Science in Information Technology Project Management from Robert Morris University. His IT career began before college and he's held various IT positions with local businesses since. He has gained extensive knowledge and experience working with both large and small companies.
